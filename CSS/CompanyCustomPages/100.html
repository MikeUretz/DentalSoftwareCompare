Brightsquid Dental Link is a HIPAA-compliant online platform for information and image exchange between dentists, specialists and laboratories. To simplify the process of communicating patient data and images, this platform provides a secure way to communicate across organizations. Unlike email, which is not HIPAA compliant, Brightsquid allows dentists and specialists to send and receive patient-centric information. Referrals produce alerts to increase communication and scheduling. In addition, a dashboard of cases can be built for dentists and dental labs. Collaboration and up-to-date information are all available in real time. Dental images can be viewed in a centralized location and annotated by authorized users.

As a web-based program, Dental Link works on all operating systems.
